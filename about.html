About Me

Hello, I am Walter Philip Dych, a recent graduate from the University of Delaware with a B.A. in Linguistics and a minor in European Studies. Language has played a huge part in my life; whether it was in my home where Dutch, German, English and Frisian were spoken, on the football pitch where languages from all over like Spanish, Yoruba, French, among others, or on my various journeys in different parts of the world, I have always been facinated with languages and the way in which they shape the world around us.
