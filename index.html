About me

I am the Laboratory Manager of the Lab for Auditory Cognitive Neuroscience at Binghamton University. I am currently interested in the interaction between language and gesture, and how these two modalities interact in the brain. I am also interested in the role of gesture in communication and how it can be used to enhance communication.

What i'm doing

  • Methodological Development

    I have been working on developing new methods for the coding of multimodal data, including co-speech gestures and speech.

  • Programming

    I am also focused on developing opensource tools for data collection and analysis in the lab and in the field.
